Addressing Learner Bullying and School Contact Information


Ensuring the safety and well-being f our students is a top priority at LearnersGuid learnrsGuide. It has come to our attention that instances of learner bullying have been reported, and we want to address this issue promptly and effectively.

Bullying in any form is unacceptable and goes against the values of our school community. We are committed to creating a safe and inclusive environment where all learners feel respected, valued, and supported. It is crucial for us to work together to address and prevent bullying behavior.
